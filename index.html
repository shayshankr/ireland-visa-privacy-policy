Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 May 2026

1. Overview

Ireland Visa Checker ("the App") is developed and maintained by Shayshank Rathore ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights in relation to it.

By using the App you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects no personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or log in.
  • We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying details.
  • We do not collect or store the application numbers you enter.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or analytics trackers.

3. How the App Works

When you enter an application number, it is sent over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our backend server solely to query official Irish Embassy decision records published on ireland.ie. The number is used only to perform this lookup and is not logged, stored, or shared.

The backend server fetches publicly available data directly from the Irish Government website. We do not modify or interpret this data beyond formatting it for display in the App.

4. Data Storage

No user data is stored on our servers or on your device beyond what is needed to display your search result within the current session. When you close the App, no data is retained.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party infrastructure:

  • HuggingFace Spaces — hosts our backend API server. HuggingFace may log standard HTTP request metadata (IP address, timestamp, request path) for operational purposes. See HuggingFace's Privacy Policy.
  • ireland.ie — the source of all visa decision data. We only read publicly available pages; we do not interact with ireland.ie on your behalf.

We do not integrate any advertising, social media, or analytics SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
