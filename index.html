Privacy Policy

Ireland Visa Application Status Checker  ·  New Delhi Embassy

Overview

The Ireland Visa Application Status Checker is a free tool that helps applicants instantly check the status of their Irish visa application processed by the New Delhi Embassy. This policy explains what data we access, what we do not collect, and how we handle your use of the app.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your application number never leaves your browser session.

Data We Access

The app fetches publicly available visa decision data published by the Irish Embassy, New Delhi:

  • Source: ireland.ie – Visa Processing Times & Decisions
  • This file is published by the Irish Government and is freely accessible to everyone.
  • The file contains application numbers and their decision status only — no names or personal details.
  • It is refreshed automatically every hour from the source.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not ask for your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not store the application numbers you search for.
  • We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics services.
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

File Uploads

The app offers an optional manual upload of an ODS or Excel file as a fallback if the automatic fetch is unavailable. Any file you upload is processed entirely within your active browser session and is never transmitted to any server or stored anywhere.

CSV Downloads

You may download the full dataset as a CSV file. This is the same publicly available data sourced from ireland.ie. No personal information about you is included in this file.

Third-Party Data Source

All visa decision data is owned and published by the Department of Foreign Affairs, Ireland. We have no affiliation with the Irish Government or Embassy. We simply display data that is already publicly available at:

ireland.ie/en/india/newdelhi/services/visas/processing-times-and-decisions/

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
