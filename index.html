📋 1. Overview & Summary

Short version: This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you. The application number you search is never sent to any server we operate or own. There are no accounts, no cookies, no analytics, and no advertising.

This Privacy Policy explains in detail how the Ireland Visa Application Status Checker ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use the application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about our data practices.

🛂 2. About This Application

The Ireland Visa Application Status Checker is a free, open-source tool that helps visa applicants check the status of their Irish visa application. The app fetches publicly available visa decision data published by Irish embassies and allows users to search for their application number without leaving their browser.

The app currently supports visa decisions published by the following Irish embassies and visa offices:

Visa Office Official Data Source
Abu Dhabi (UAE) ireland.ie – Abu Dhabi weekly decision reports
Abuja (Nigeria) ireland.ie – Abuja weekly decision reports
Ankara (Türkiye) ireland.ie – Ankara weekly decision report
Beijing (China) ireland.ie – Beijing visa decisions
London (Great Britain) ireland.ie – London visa processing times & decision reports
Moscow (Russia) ireland.ie – Moscow visas for Ireland
New Delhi (India) ireland.ie – New Delhi processing times & decisions
South Africa irishimmigration.ie – South Africa visa desk
General (all offices) irishimmigration.ie – Visa decisions

All data sourced by this application is owned and published by the Irish Government or Irish Immigration Service. This policy covers all current and future embassy or visa office pages supported within the application. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Irish Government, the Department of Foreign Affairs Ireland, Irish Immigration Service, or any Irish Embassy or Consulate.

📂 3. Data We Access (Public Government Data)

The app retrieves visa decision data that is publicly published by the Irish Embassy. This data is freely accessible to anyone on the internet and is owned entirely by the Irish Government.

Data Source Contains Personal Info?
Visa application numbers and their decision status (approved / refused / pending) ireland.ie (Irish Government official website) No – numbers only, no names or contact details

The data file is published by the embassy and refreshed periodically. The app retrieves this file directly from the official government URL each time it is used and does not store a private copy on any server we control.

🚫 4. Data We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do not collect any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other contact information
  • Passport numbers, date of birth, nationality, or immigration details
  • The application numbers you search for
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or hardware information
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Browsing history or cross-site tracking data
  • Photos, videos, contacts, or any other device content
  • Financial information of any kind
No personal data is ever sent to, or stored on, any server operated by this application. All search activity happens exclusively within your browser session.

🔍 5. How Your Application Number Is Handled

When you type an application number into the search field, that number is processed entirely within your device's browser. It is compared locally against the publicly available government dataset that was already loaded into your session.

Your application number is never transmitted to any server, database, or third party. It is not logged, cached externally, or retained in any way after your browser session ends.

📁 6. Optional File Uploads

The app includes an optional feature allowing you to manually upload an ODS or Excel (.xlsx) file as a fallback when the automatic data fetch is unavailable.

  • Any file you upload is processed entirely within your active browser session.
  • The file is never transmitted to any server or external service.
  • The file is not stored, cached, or retained after your session ends.
  • We recommend only uploading files obtained from the official ireland.ie government website.

🌐 7. Hosting & Infrastructure

This application is hosted on GitHub Pages, a static website hosting service provided by GitHub, Inc. (a subsidiary of Microsoft Corporation). When you visit the application, GitHub's servers may automatically collect standard web server log data, which may include:

  • Your IP address (handled by GitHub's infrastructure, not by us)
  • Browser type and version
  • Date and time of the request
  • The URL of the page requested

This data is collected and controlled by GitHub, not by us. We do not access, receive, or store this server log data. For details on how GitHub handles this information, please review the GitHub Privacy Statement.

🔗 8. Third-Party Services

The app makes direct HTTP requests to the following official Irish Government websites to retrieve publicly available visa decision files. These are the only external network requests initiated by the application:

  • ireland.ie – Irish Embassy visa decision data (Abu Dhabi, Abuja, Ankara, Beijing, London, Moscow, New Delhi)
  • irishimmigration.ie – Irish Immigration Service visa decision data (South Africa, general decisions)

We do not integrate any of the following:

  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Firebase)
  • Social media tracking pixels or SDKs
  • Crash reporting or performance monitoring services
  • Payment processors
  • Authentication or identity providers
When the app fetches data from ireland.ie, the Irish Government's web server may log your IP address as part of standard server logging. This is governed by the Ireland.ie Privacy Statement, not by this policy.

🍪 9. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

This application does not use:

  • Cookies of any kind (session, persistent, or third-party)
  • Web beacons, pixels, or tracking tags
  • Browser fingerprinting techniques
  • Local storage or IndexedDB for user tracking
  • Cross-site tracking mechanisms

Your browser may use its own local cache to store the page for faster reloading. This is standard browser behaviour outside our control and does not involve any tracking.
